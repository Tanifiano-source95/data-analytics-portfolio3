## Project 02 - Labour Market Analysis - Benin

### Overview
Analysis of unemployment rates across 5 regions 
of Benin by sector, using Google Sheets 
Pivot Tables and data visualization.

### Tools Used
- Google Sheets
- Pivot Table
- Bar Chart
- Formulas : AVERAGE, MAX, MIN, COUNTIF, SUMIF

### Key Findings

1. Mono region has the highest unemployment rate
   at 21.99% - likely driven by high illiteracy 
   rates and limited access to formal education.

2. Atlantique region has the lowest unemployment 
   rate at 13.75% - major cities concentration 
   provides more job opportunities for 
   educated workers.

3. Borgou shows moderate unemployment at 33%
   mainly in the agriculture sector.

### Recommendations

1. Urbanization policy : develop economic zones
   in high-unemployment regions to attract 
   businesses and create local jobs.

2. Education investment : increase school 
   enrollment rates for youth and provide 
   vocational training for adults to reduce
   illiteracy rates.

3. Private sector incentives : encourage 
   companies to relocate or open branches 
   in underserved regions.

### Skills Demonstrated
- Data cleaning and formatting
- Statistical analysis (AVERAGE, SUMIF, COUNTIF)
- Pivot Table creation
- Data visualization
- Critical thinking and policy recommendations
